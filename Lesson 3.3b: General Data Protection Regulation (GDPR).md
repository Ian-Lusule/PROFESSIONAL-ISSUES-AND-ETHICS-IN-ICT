## 1. ðŸŒ Background: A Global Standard
Implemented on **May 25, 2018**, the GDPR replaced outdated 1995 laws to address modern threats like big data, social media profiling, and massive data breaches. 

**Why does it matter in Kenya?**
The GDPR has **Extra-territorial Effect**. This means if a Kenyan tourism company or a local tech startup processes the data of someone living in the EU, that Kenyan company **must** comply with GDPR or face massive international fines.

## 2. âš–ï¸ The 7 Core Principles of GDPR
These principles are the "North Star" for any ICT professional designing a system.



| Principle | Professional Requirement |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Lawfulness, Fairness, Transparency** | Don't hide what you are doing with data in 50-page legal documents. |
| **Purpose Limitation** | If you collect an email for a password reset, don't use it for a newsletter later. |
| **Data Minimization** | Don't ask for "Gender" or "Home Address" if you're just building a weather app. |
| **Accuracy** | Give users a way to update their info if it changes. |
| **Storage Limitation** | Set an "expiry date" for data. Delete inactive accounts after a certain period. |
| **Integrity & Confidentiality** | Use encryption (AES-256) and secure servers to prevent leaks. |
| **Accountability** | You must be able to *prove* you followed the rules (logs, audits, DPO). |

## 3. ðŸ‘©â€ðŸ’» Rights of the Data Subject
GDPR shifts power from corporations back to the people. 



* **Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"):** You can demand a company delete all your data if it's no longer needed.
* **Right to Data Portability:** You can ask for your data in a "structured, machine-readable format" (like a .CSV) to move it to a competitor.
* **Automated Decision-Making:** You have the right to demand a **human review** if an AI (like a loan-approval bot) rejects your application.

## 4. ðŸ¢ Obligations of Organizations
As an ICT professional, you are responsible for implementing these "By Design":

1.  **Privacy by Design:** Privacy shouldn't be an "add-on." It must be part of the initial code architecture.
2.  **72-Hour Breach Rule:** If your database is hacked, you have exactly **72 hours** to notify the regulator.
3.  **Data Protection Officer (DPO):** Large organizations must hire a dedicated expert to oversee privacy.
