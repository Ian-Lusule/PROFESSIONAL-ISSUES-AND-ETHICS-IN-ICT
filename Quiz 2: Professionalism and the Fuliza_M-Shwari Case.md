## Lesson Summary: The Fraud Triangle in ICT



[Image of the Fraud Triangle: Pressure, Opportunity, and Rationalization]


The Fuliza case can be analyzed through the **Fraud Triangle**:
1. **Opportunity:** Weak KYC and fraud detection systems in Telcos.
2. **Pressure:** Financial gain for students.
3. **Rationalization/Lack of Ethics:** Using specialized knowledge to exploit "faceless" systems without considering the societal cost.
